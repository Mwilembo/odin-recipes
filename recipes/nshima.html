Nshima



Description


Nshima is eaten with your hands and often dipped in relishes of meat, beans, and vegetables. Bring a taste of traditional Zambian cooking into your kitchen with the following recipes.


Ingredients


  • Mealie Meal

  • Water


Steps


  1. Grab a pot

  2. Add Water

  3. Put your pot on the stove and turn it on

  4. As the water is getting heated, add your Mealie Meal and create a paste-like mixture

  5. Let it boil

  6. After 10 mins start add more Mealie Meal

  7. let it rest for 10 more mins after it has reached your desired texture
